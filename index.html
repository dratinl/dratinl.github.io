About me

As an individual I have always been passionate about learning and development, and I combine this with a strong curiosity for all things tech. While I strive when planning for multiple contingincies, I have always maintained that the best way to learn something new is, like a bird, to throw yourself into the deep end and 'Learn by Doing'.

A supporting role allows me to function in both a student and teaching role, often taking technical issues and terms and simplifying into basic terms for either myself, clients or other team members. As a hobby and in my free time I take to coaching Youth Soccer Teams in my local community with a focus on teamwork and self improvement. Teamwork and collaboration are ingrained in my core values and it is something I am passionate about in both professional and casual environments.

The thought process, method of finding answers and freedom of solution drew me to the field.

One thing I realized the more I dove into the subject is how applicable programming and computer science is to all aspects of learning. It provides a different method to learning how to use tools and functions as a means to an end, and is an intuitive way to understand cutting up a difficult problem into smaller steps. In today's age, I believe it could be a fundamental subject to learn for kids as the concepts help directly with mathematic and general scientific problem solving.
